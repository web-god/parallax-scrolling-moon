Tum kab aa rahe ho mulaqaat ke liye?
Maine chaand roke rakha hai ek raat ke liye.
Chandni raat ki khushboo hai teri yaadon ki,
Beet gaye lamhe tere intezaar mein rote-rote.
Teri aahat ka besabab intezaar hai dil mein,
Chand se mera rishta hai, adhura, anokha sa.
          
Chaand se poochta hoon, kya woh tera aashiq hai?
Jo har raat teri yaadon mein khoya rehta hai.
Dil ko sukoon milta hai teri muskurahat se,
Chaand pe tera chehra hai, chamakta, haseen sa.
          
Tumse milne ki raat hai, aasmaan bhi bekaraar hai,
Maine chaand roke rakha hai ek raat ke liye.
Zameen pe bekhauf uth rahi hai mohabbat ki sada,
Tum aaoge mere dil ki dhadkan bane, woh saath lekar.
          
Chaandni ki roshni mein tera intezaar hai,
Dil mein hai ummeed, saath leke aayega tu.
Raat ki raahon mein badhta hai mere khwaab ka silsila,
Maine chaand roke rakha hai, bas tera intezaar hai.
          
Tum kab aa rahe ho mulaqaat ke liye?
Maine chaand roke rakha hai ek raat ke liye.
Aa jaao mere saath, chaandni raat mein kho jayein,
Dil se dil milakar, pyaar ki baat keh jayein.
          
Chaand se hoga humara milan,
Aasmaan ki oonchaiyon tak le jayenge humein.
Raat ki tanhai mein saath chaleinge haathon mein haath,
Maine chaand roke rakha hai, bas intezaar hai tera.
          
Tumse milne ki raat hai, aasmaan bhi bekaraar hai,
Maine chaand roke rakha hai ek raat ke liye.
Aaoge toh sajegi duniya, hogi khushiyaan saath,
Chaand pe likhi hogi hamari kismat ki raat.
